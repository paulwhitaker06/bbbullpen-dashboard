Methodology & sources

Bullpen rankings

Three forward-looking ingredients, each normalized 0–100 across the 30 teams, then averaged: LI-weighted projected FIP (closer 1.7×, primary setup 1.4×, secondary setup 1.2×, middle 1.0×); projected ROS WAR (Steamer/ZiPS blend, captures depth); current-season Pitching+ (Stuff+ × Location+).

Phase 2 — Editorial + availability layer

ESPN closer org chart parses each team's IL list and editorial section. Razzball pitch-count chart tags pitchers as "tired" via the deterministic ESPN formula (≥25 pitches yesterday, ≥35 over 3 days, or back-to-back appearances). Both layers REMOVE pitchers from the top-5 before composite math, then refill from FG slots 6-8. Watchlist captures every removal.

Phase 3 — Pitcher List math layer

RP Roundup parser scans editorial sentences for Tier A signals (velocity/stuff/command decline → 0.7× LI-weight discount, single mention triggers) and Tier B (concerning/shaky/wobbly → requires 2+ mentions in 7 days). Recovery patterns (welcome back, first save since) undo active discounts. Closed-set pitcher-name matching against the FG roster. Persistence log at pl_history.jsonl.

Phase 4 — Key Games + Kalshi value

Swing Score = 0.90 × BE + 0.10 × PP

BE = composite gap. PP = 0.70 × PP_vegas + 0.30 × PP_starter. PP_vegas uses a U-shape (trough at total=8.5, peaks at low/high totals — leverage in close games, volume in slugfests). Threshold ≥ 60 for KEY tag.

v1.6.0 Kalshi value: our_prob = 0.5 + (BE / 100) × K with K = 0.10. Compare to Kalshi effective probability (post-fee). Value ≥ +4% → STRONG; +2-4% → MARGINAL; below → SKIP.

Phase 5 — Opponent + same-day adjustments + dynamic refill

Opponent-offense modifier (×0.95 to ×1.05 on BE) based on opposing team's 30-day wRC+ vs L/R weighted by the BE-favored bullpen's handedness mix. PL Streamer Rankings parsed for "no [Closer] today" signals (dynamic top-8 refill, recompute LI-FIP) and "left-handed pockets" (+3 handedness). Tonight's games only.

Phase 7 status — empirical K calibration

Tier 1 (4,857 games, 2024+2025): naive K = 0.182, 95% CI [0.14, 0.22]. Initial reaction: K=0.10 is too low. Tier 2 (with team-strength controls): K_LPM collapses to −0.006, 95% CI [−0.10, +0.09]. Bullpen edge has essentially zero independent predictive value once offense + rotation gap are controlled. Tier 2.3 (light) robustness: VIF on BE = 1.77 (no multicollinearity); close-games filter doesn't rescue the signal. Production K stays at 0.10 pending real-world bet tracking; "value %" labels treated as directional only.
