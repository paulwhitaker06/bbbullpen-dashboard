Methodology

how the numbers compute

Composite = mean of three normalized scores: LI-weighted FIP (lower better), team RP WAR, team Pitching+. Each metric normalized 0–100 across the 30 teams.

LI weights: top-5 in leverage order × [1.7, 1.4, 1.2, 1.0, 1.0]. Phase 3 PL discounts multiply this by 0.7 for flagged pitchers.

Phase 2 removes IL/tired pitchers and refills from FG slots 6-8 before composite math. Phase 5b applies same-day removal+refill for "no [Closer] today" PL signals.

Swing Score = 0.90 × |BE_adjusted| + 0.10 × PP, where BE = composite gap between teams and PP = 0.7 × U-shape vegas + 0.3 × clamp(avg starter ERA). Threshold for KEY = 60.

BE adjustment (Phase 5): opponent-offense modifier ×0.95 to ×1.05 based on opposing wRC+ vs L/R weighted by pen handedness mix.

Kalshi value: our_prob − Kalshi_post_fee_implied_prob. our_prob = 0.5 + (BE / 100) × K, K = 0.10 (Phase 7 to calibrate empirically). Threshold for STRONG = +4%, MARGINAL = +2%.
